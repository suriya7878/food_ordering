About A2B RESTAURANT

A2B RESTAURANT is dedicated to serving the best of Tamil Nadu's rich and diverse cuisine—from tiffin classics like idli, dosa, and pongal, to flavourful gravies, authentic Chettinad, and beloved sweets.

Since 1995, we’ve celebrated the culinary traditions of Tamil Nadu, delighting diners of all ages! Our chefs use traditional recipes and the freshest local ingredients to bring you an authentic taste of South India.

We believe that food is more than just sustenance; it's a celebration of culture, family, and community. Every dish at A2B RESTAURANT is prepared with passion and served with warmth, ensuring a memorable dining experience.

We invite you to visit, taste tradition, and celebrate every meal as a festival.
